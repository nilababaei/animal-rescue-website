-Sponsorship Levels-

“Friend” $50.00

Choice of available animals and a photo of your chosen animal will be posted to our “Sponsorships” website page for one month with your name and an optional message.

“Guardian “ $100.00

Choice of available animals, personalized certificate of sponsorship and a ready to frame photo of your sponsored animal. A photo of your chosen animal will be posted to our "Sponsorships" website page for one month with your name and an optional message. These sponsorship levels are a great gift for a birthday, graduation, anniversary or for a memorial tribute.

"Protector" $1000.00

Everything in other levels PLUS: A sign on your sponsored animal's home enclosure with your name as sponsor. Protector status sponsorships have exclusive sponsorship rights to that animal for that year. Protector level sponsors may come for one private tour with a guest. Additional guests can be added to the tour for $50.00 each.
